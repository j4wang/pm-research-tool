# Competitive Research Questions - Deeper Dive

## Questions

# Strategic positioning

1. What customer segments or use cases are competitors explicitly targeting vs. avoiding?
2. How are competitors positioning against each other — who are they naming as the enemy?

# Go-to-market

3. What channels are competitors using to acquire customers (PLG, sales-led, partnerships)?
4. How long is the typical sales cycle and who is the buyer vs. the user?

# Product trajectory

5. What have competitors shipped in the last 6-12 months, and what does it signal about their direction?
6. Are competitors moving up-market, down-market, or expanding into adjacent categories?

# Business health signals

7. What does headcount growth or contraction signal about each competitor's momentum? (LinkedIn is useful for this)


## Output format
<!-- Describe how you want the research brief structured -->
Produce a structured competitive brief with an executive summary, a competitor-by-competitor breakdown, and a final section on gaps and opportunities.
