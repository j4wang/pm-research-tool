# Competitive Research Questions

## Questions

1. Who are the top 3-5 competitors in this space, and what are their core value propositions?

2. What pricing models do competitors use (freemium, seat-based, usage-based)?

3. What features appear consistently across competitors, and what gaps exist?

4. What are users saying about competitors — common praise and common complaints?

5. Are there any recent launches, funding announcements, or strategic moves worth noting?

6. What does the ideal customer profile look like for this category?

7. What are the competitive moats for each competitor in this category?

## Output format
<!-- Describe how you want the research brief structured -->
Produce a structured competitive brief with an executive summary, a competitor-by-competitor breakdown, and a final section on gaps and opportunities.
